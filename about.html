About SteamTeen

What is STEAMTEEN?

STEAMTEEN is a national conference dedicated to exposing teenagers to STEAM fields. We primarily cater to teenagers ages 13-18, but anyone is welcome to register and attend a conference! While we do accept donations, our conferences are 100% free for ALL participants. The only things that are required in order to be fully immersed in the events are internet connection, access to Zoom, and a passion for learning! This year, our 2024 virtual conference will be held on Zoom from July 6th to July 8th. Visit our sign up page or click here to register!




Want to Work at STEAMTEEN?

At STEAMTEEN, we offer a unique opportunity to be at the forefront of empowering teenagers. Joining our team means becoming part of a passionate community dedicated to inspiring the next generation of thinkers, creators, and leaders. Whether you're driven by a love for education, technology, or making a meaningful impact in young lives, STEAMTEEN provides a platform for you to contribute your skills and expertise towards a noble cause.
