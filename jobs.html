What We Are Looking For

Here at STEAMTEEN, we are looking for applicants that demonstrate great love and respect for all STEAM-related fields. Applicants should be excited to learn and grow all while mentoring teenagers that are interested in following in their footsteps. Does this sound like you? Apply now!

Interns

Join us at STEAMTEEN, where we're dedicated to inspiring the next generation of innovators through virtual conferences focused on STEAM subjects. We're excited to offer an internship opportunity that contributes to our mission in unique and impactful ways.

STEAM Curriculum Development Internship:

➡Collaborate on creating engaging lesson plans, workshops, and digital resources.

➡Use STEAM concepts to make learning interesting for students of all ages.

➡Conduct research and gather resources.

➡Contribute creative ideas to shape the future of STEAM education.

Monitors

Virtual Conference

➡Oversee technical aspects of virtual conferences, ensuring a seamless experience for participants.

➡Manage participant engagement, including Q&A sessions.

➡Troubleshoot technical glitches and address any issues that arise during the event.

➡Proactive and quick problem-solving skills are essential.

Secretary

➡Be the heart of STEAMTEEN operations, facilitating communication and coordination.

➡Maintain accurate meeting records, handle correspondence, and assist in event planning/execution.

➡Utilize meticulous organization and communication skills to keep the team informed and connected.

Speakers

➡Share groundbreaking findings or current projects in a specific STEM field.

➡Deliver a captivating talk to ignite passion for STEM in attendees and be open to new thoughts and ideas from others.

➡Provide in-depth knowledge and insights on a particular STEM topic.

➡Moderate panels, workshops, or Q&A sessions to foster engagement.

➡Discuss career opportunities and experiences within STEM fields.

Treasurer

➡Manage budgets and track expenses for STEAMTEEN.

➡Develop financial strategies to support STEAM education initiatives and determine budgets for future STEAM showcases and events.

➡Ensure the financial health and stability of the organization.

➡Contribute to the sustainability of STEAMTEEN's programs.
