Frequently Asked Questions

When does registration for STEAMTEEN 2024 close?

Registration for the National STEAMTEEN Conference 2024 closes Tuesday, May 31, 2024. Make sure that you sign up before then to claim your spot!

What are the dates for the National STEAMTEEN Conference 2025?

The dates for the National STEAMTEEN Conference 2025 will be posted in December 2024 and registration will open on January 1, 2025.

How do I know if I am elligible to register and attend a STEAMTEEN conference?

While STEAMTEEN primarily caters to teenagers ages 13-18, anyone is welcome to register and attend a conference! We accept everyone regardless of their background and promote a safe environment for all. The only things that are required in order to attain a full STEAMTEEN experience are internet connection, access to Zoom, and a passion for learning!

I already attended a STEAMTEEN conference in the past, can I still attend another conference?

Absolutely! There is no limit to how many times you can attend a STEAMTEEN conference. While we do stay true to our STEAM values, our guest speakers and activities vary by year so you will be guaranteed a new and interesting experience every time!

Why should I specifically work at STEAMTEEN?

At STEAMTEEN, you'll be part of a team dedicated to inspiring and educating the next generation of innovators, directly impacting young learners' lives by empowering them to explore STEAM subjects. We foster a collaborative and inclusive culture where every voice is valued, providing opportunities for growth and development in event management, content creation, technology integration, and more. Working here is a fulfilling experience where you can align your passion with purpose, witnessing the positive impact of your contributions on teenagers' lives and their excitement for learning. Join us and be part of something truly meaningful!
